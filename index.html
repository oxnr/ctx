Thesis

AI-Native Agencies

Agencies have always been hard to scale. Low margins, slow manual work, and the only way to grow is to add more people.

AI changes this. Instead of selling advice and hoping clients execute, we use the models ourselves and deliver finished work — at a fraction of the time and multiples of the quality.

Agencies of the future will look more like software companies, with software margins. We're building that now, focused on the agentic programming stack: Claude Code, Codex, Windsurf, Cursor, and the open tools emerging around them.

Our thesis: context is the durable asset. Portable, reusable context artifacts — not rigid platform dependencies — are what survive each generation of tools.
